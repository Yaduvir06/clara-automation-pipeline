Agent: And what about after-hours calls? Do you get emergency calls?

Agent: How do you currently route those calls?

Client: Right now, if it's an emergency after hours, the answering service patches them through to our on-call tech. The number is 555-0100. During business hours, we transfer to our dispatch department.

Agent: Do you use any specific software systems?

Client: Yeah, we use ServiceTrade for work orders and scheduling. But here's the thing - we don't want emergency sprinkler calls creating tickets in ServiceTrade automatically. Those need to go straight to the on-call person.

Agent: Got it. What happens if the transfer doesn't go through?

Client: That's a problem we have now. If the on-call tech doesn't answer, callers just get stuck. We need the system to take their info and promise a callback within 15 minutes.

Agent: Makes sense. Anything else important?

Client: We're located at 123 Main Street in Springfield, Illinois. And we get a lot of false alarms, so the agent should probably confirm it's a real emergency before routing.
